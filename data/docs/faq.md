# Product Support FAQ

## How do I reset my password?
Go to the login page, click **Forgot Password**, and follow the email reset link.

## How do I update my billing method?
Navigate to **Settings > Billing** and click **Update Payment Method**.

## What if I cannot access my account email?
Contact support and provide your account ID plus proof of ownership. We will verify and help recover access.

## What is your refund policy?
Monthly plans can be refunded within 14 days of the latest charge date if usage is below policy thresholds.
