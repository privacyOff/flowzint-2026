# Incident Management

## Severity Levels

### Severity 1

Complete service outage affecting multiple customers.

### Severity 2

Major functionality unavailable.

### Severity 3

Minor degradation with workaround available.

### Severity 4

Cosmetic issues or documentation defects.

## Incident Lifecycle

1. Detection
2. Investigation
3. Mitigation
4. Resolution
5. Postmortem
