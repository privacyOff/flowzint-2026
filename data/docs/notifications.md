# Notification System

## Email Notifications

Users can receive:

- Ticket updates
- Security alerts
- Billing reminders

## In-App Notifications

Notifications appear in the dashboard notification center.

## Notification Delays

Most notifications are delivered within 60 seconds.

## Troubleshooting

If notifications are missing:

1. Verify notification settings.
2. Check spam folders.
3. Confirm email verification status.
