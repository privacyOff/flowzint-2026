# Refund Policy

## Monthly Plans

Refund requests must be submitted within 14 days of the latest charge.

## Annual Plans

Refund eligibility is reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

## Non-Refundable Charges

The following are generally non-refundable:

- Professional services
- Training packages
- Custom development work

## Processing Time

Approved refunds typically appear within 5–10 business days.
